(Haki literally means: Ambition)

“Haki' is a power that lies dormant in all the world's creatures. 'Presence', 'fighting spirit' and 'intimidation.' It is not different from the things that humans can naturally sense such as these. 'The act of not doubting'. That is strength!”

Haki is an energy based power system in the anime/manga series "One Piece". Haki is a mysterious power that allows the user to utilize their own spiritual energy for various purposes. It can be used to sense people's spiritual energy, grant the user armour-like skin, and intimidate others. All living beings in the world are capable of learning Haki; however, most people do not notice it or fail to awaken it.

There are 3 types of Haki: Armament, Observation, and Conqueror's

*How Haki sounds:

What is Armament Haki (Busoshoku no Haki)?

"It protects you, much like invisible armor, and it increases the strength of your attacks, a powerful ability!"

Armament Haki is a form of Haki that allows the user to use their own spiritual energy to create, in essence, an invisible armor around themself, providing incredible offensive and defensive capabilities.

Have I ever used Armament Haki?

When practicing Taekwondo, I use Armament Haki to buff up my body and defend myself from my training partner's powerful, incoming kicks!

What is Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku no Haki)?

"You will now reply, 'I see...that's fine then."

Observation Haki is a form of Haki that grants the user a sixth sense that allows them to sense the presence, strength, and emotions of others, and can even let the user see into the future.

Have I ever used Observation Haki?

I often use this in dodgeball games to instanteneously dodge balls thrown at me given enough distance. I'm able to tell when I will be faked out with a ball vs actually being attacked.

What is Conqueror's Haki (Haoshoku no Haki)?

"This man... He has the 'qualities of a king', the disposition to stand above others...!!!"

Conqueror's Haki is a rare form of Haki that allows the user to exert their own willpower over others. This type of Haki cannot be attained through training and only one in several million people are born with this ability. It is said that whoever possesses this type of Haki has the qualities of a king.

Have I ever used Conqueror's Haki?

My enthusiasm in playing Capture the Flag often awakens my Conqueror's! I often glare menacingly at my opponents and scare them from coming to my side of the field to steal my flags.
