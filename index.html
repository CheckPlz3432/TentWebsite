As the CTA is preparing to extend the Red Line south to 130th Street in Chicago, one must wonder what other additions and alternation can be made to the city's transit system to make it more accessible. A draft made by David Sotokarlin exemplifies a new CTA train line, derived from the existing Metra Electric Line called the CTA Lime Line, providing cheap, high speed transit to the citizens of the East Side of Chicago.
