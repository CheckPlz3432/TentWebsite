Brown Line Extension: This chiefly wouldn't work be

We wouldn't be able to realistically imlement the Brown Line extension The reason we can't is because we have to dig into the already narrow street of Lawrence Ave and dig under for subways, on top of delaying traffic even more than it already is, which would cost millions of dollars and at minimum 2 years to complete. Plus, WHY extend the Brown Line when there's already a Lawrence Ave bus available? The Lime Line extension is making use of transit that already exists. Turning Metra into CTA for an underserved part of Chicago, like the Red Line Extension, could be a great opportunity to being business and traffic to far off neighborhoods while at the same time making use of resources we have already.
